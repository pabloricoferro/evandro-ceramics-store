P+3 is an independent ceramics practice working between workshops in Colombia and Italy. Every object begins as an inquiry — into texture, weight, silence, and the particular memory of a material. No molds. No repetition. Each piece is resolved when it demands to be.

Glaze recipes are developed in-house from local mineral sources. Firings are conducted in small batches to maintain complete control over each surface outcome. We offer a limited number of commissions per year for collectors who require specific dimensions or palette.
