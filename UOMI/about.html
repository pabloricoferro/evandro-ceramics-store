About UOMI

UOMI creates ceramic art pieces designed as wall compositions and collectible objects. Every piece is made in small batches with a focus on texture, detail, and permanence.

Our process combines hand shaping, glazing, and high-temperature firing to create works that feel both contemporary and timeless.
